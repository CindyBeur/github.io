About

I am a French-American, currently living in the United States. I have worked in education for the last 20 years with students at all levels - elementary, secondary, and adult learners. I am excited to bring my unique skill set to help you meet your language goals.

  • 2009-2012

    Immersion Beginnings

    The state of Utah has over 100 dual immersion programs in its public schools, the highest number in the nation. These programs provide students with the opportunity to learn in both English and a second language, fostering bilingualism and biliteracy. I was fortunate to be a part of this movement from the beginning as one of their first French immersion teachers.

  • 2012-2017

    A New Chapter

    In 2012, I moved to California to teach at the newly opened Santa Rosa French American Charter School. During this time, I expanded my teaching repertoire and specialized language programs. I also began to develop my own curriculum materials, focusing on real-world language use and cultural competence.

  • 2017-Present

    Secondary Level & Adult Learners

    In 2017, I began to work with secondary students and adult learners, focusing on advanced language acquisition and cultural competence. This shift allowed me to explore new pedagogical approaches and collaborate with a diverse range of learners.

  • June 2024

    Immersion Week Trial

    In June 2024, three adult students joined me for the first immersion week experience in the Burgundy Region of France.The aim was to provide intensive language and cultural experiences for participants. This initiative seeks to deepen learners' engagement and proficiency through real-world practice and interaction.
